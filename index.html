Biography

Xingjian Diao is a first year Master Student of Computer Science at Northwestern University. He received his B.S. degree from University of Pittsburgh (2020) in Computer Science. His research interests lie within the areas of data management (database, Big data), scalable machine learning, and transfer learning. Before joined Northwestern, He worked as an intern at University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center in Dallas. During the internship, he enhanced the hospital’s manual data analytics process by building a multi-species database on MySQL and designing a user-friendly application which synthesized multiple algorithms for the computational analysis of genomics and proteomics data via RStudio. Diao said, "I have a great love for computational science—it is especially interesting for me to see how large amounts of data are processed and transformed into usable models or insights. "
